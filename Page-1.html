N2 Infra Technologies

N2 Infrastructure has targeted the Kittyhawk Heaviside Aircraft model because it allows instant implementation into the N2 Infrastructure application and processes. Kittyhawk is currently building a remotely-piloted electric aircraft and is intending to make air taxis affordable and eco-conscious. With the perks of being remotely piloted- the aircraft will be commanded by a ground operator overseeing multiple vehicles, whilst also handling maintenance during the pick-up portion of the application. There will be charging stations for the electric aircraft along the way that are solar panel charged. N2 Infrastructure aims to tackle the challenges mentioned above by providing a flight-based delivery service; the company will be renting an eVTOL aircraft and shall hire pilots to operate them. By using this aircraft, the company will reduce the cost to $2/kg. Customers can use an app to access a plane that will provide them with these goods or services. The customers will also have a 24-hour time delivery window after their order is placed. The company will compile rankings at its transport node and send out deliveries the following day. The customers can also have their cargo dropped to the transport nodes for N2 Infrastructure to do the last mile delivery. For convenience and accessibility, the node is also where the aircraft would be stationed.
