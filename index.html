Overview

N2 Infra is creating remote aerial delivery of items in Canada. Customers will be able to push a button and get a Delivery  on-demand.

Tracking

In Remote areas of Canada deliveries of medical supplies and essential commodities during adverse weather and winter is next to impossible via road, N2 Infra is bridging the gap and making delivery of supplies much easier.

N2 Infra is partenering with large drone/air taxi companies  for delivery of goods, the drone needs much smaller space to land and high speed delivery, making deliveries possible much faster.
