N2 Infra
Node to Node Delivery

Overview

N2 Infra is creating remote aerial delivery of items in Canada. Customers will be able to push a button and get a Delivery  on-demand.

Tracking

In Remote areas of Canada deliveries of medical supplies and essential commodities during adverse weather and winter is next to impossible via road, N2 Infra is bridging the gap and making delivery of supplies much easier. 

Sky Delivery

N2 Infra has paternered with Heaviside Kitty hawk for delivery of goods, the drone needs just 30 X 30  sqft space to land and operates at 180 km/h speed , making deliveries possible much faster.

Finger-Tip 

N2 Infra Monile application makes the booking and tracking of goods delivery from your finger-tip to your door-step.
